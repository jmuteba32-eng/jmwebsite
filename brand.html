01 · Fitness

Built on the Court,
Forged in the Race

Jonathan's relationship with fitness began at age five on a basketball court. He played through Revere community leagues, middle school, high school, college intramural — and was one of the founding fathers of his college club basketball program.

After college, he pursued Spartan racing and boxing — always pushing, always chasing the next challenge that demands both mental and physical toughness.
